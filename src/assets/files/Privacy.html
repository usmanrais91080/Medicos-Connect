Privacy policy

  1. General Statement:

Medicos is a separate internet world for everyone involved in the medical field. From doctors, dentists, nurses, pharmacists, physiotherapists, nutritionists, psychologists, medical technicians, and medical students to the real-world medical community at large, our unique and collaborative network benefits everyone in the healthcare ecosystem. This Privacy Policy applies to all Medicos Services that are supplied and provided. It explains how Medicos (also known as "we," "our," or "us") handles personal information about you ("you" or "your").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand how Medicos handles and protects your personal information & data. We also welcome members who have read our Privacy Policy to contact us with any questions, comments, or recommendations.

  1. Type of information we collect:

  • Things you do and information you provide:

When you use our Services, such as when you sign up for an account, produce or share content, or message or connect with others, we collect the content and other information you give. This can include details about or in the content you give, such as location or the date it was created. We also track how you use our Services, such as your interest or the people you interact with, as well as the frequency and length of your activity.

  • Networks and connections:

We gather information on the people and groups with whom you are linked, as well as how you interact with them, with whom you communicate the most and the groups with whom you like to share. If you upload, sync, or import information (such as an address book) from a device, we will also gather your contact information.

  • Device information:

Depending on the permissions you've provided, we gather information from or about the computers, phones, or other devices where you install or access medicos. We may link the data we acquire from your various devices, allowing us to provide consistent services across all your devices. Here are some samples of the data we collect from devices:

    • Operating system, hardware version, device settings, battery and signal strength, and device IDs.

    • Information about your connection, such as your mobile operator's or ISP's name, browser type, language, and time zone, as well as your mobile phone number.

    • It may include your browser type, browser version, the pages you visit, time and date visit, time spent on those pages, unique device identifiers, and other diagnostic data.

    • We may automatically collect certain information when you access the service via or through a mobile device, such as the type of mobile device, unique id, Ip address, unique device identifiers & other diagnostic data.

While using Medicos, we may ask you to give us with personally identifying information that can be used to contact or identify you. This information could include, but is not limited to:

  • Email address

  • First name and last name

  • Profile photo

  • Phone number

  • Usage data

  • CNIC or Country ID

  • Professional ID or Certificate

  • Information gathered during the use:

While using Medicos, we may collect the following information with your permission to deliver functionalities of our service:

  • Location

  • Contacts

  • Gallery

The data may be uploaded to the Medicos servers. You can enable or disable access to this information at any time using your device settings.
