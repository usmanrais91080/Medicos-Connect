FAQS

General:

  1. What is medicos connect?

The main idea behind medicos connect is to create an ecosystem for all the medical professionals around the world where different aspects of their lives i.e. personal, professional, financial and mental health can be improved. For more details on this, click below on Mission statement.

  1. Who is it for?

Anyone who has a degree or diploma in any field of medicine and healthcare is more than welcome to join in.

  1. Why is there a different profile for every module?

What you may want to share with your friends is different from what you present to a prospective employer. Different profiles for each module were designed to customize the information available for personal and professional interactions.

  1. Is Medicos only for a specific region?

It’s for our whole community worldwide.

  1. Will app be available in other languages?

Yes, slowly we will integrate other languages in the system such as Russian, Mandarin, Urdu/Hindi, Arabic and Spanish.

  1. I cannot access some functions in the app, why?

That’s because of regional restrictions imposed by certain countries and we will try to resolve them as we go, until then there’s no way around it and we apologies for that

  1. Is Medicos Connect free?

Yes, it’s completely free for individuals and colleges and but some advanced features will require you to spend ampules to unlock them.

  1. what are ampules?

Ampules are basically virtual tokens that you buy and spend in the system instead of using your credit/debit card all the time.

  1. Are ampules and wallet just like bitcoin and crypto wallet?

Ampules are not cryptocurrency.

  1. Can I update my information in the system?

Profile information can be updated anytime, except for age, gender and name. Name can only be updated before the identification process, and cannot be changed afterwards.

In case of legal name change, you will have to provide new Government identity.

  1. Why are there ads on the app?

Ads are to generate revenue for the system.

  1. Why should we enable geolocation?

In case of disabling geolocation, all the data will have to be entered manually, which can affect the functionality and efficiency of the application.

  1. Can I delete my medicos connect account?

Yes, whenever you feel like it serves you no purpose just go to settings and delete your account permanently. System will delete all your data permanently as well. Moreover, if you want to reinstall the application then you will have to go through all of the identification process again.

  1. Can I give feedback?

Yes, off course we welcome feedback and would love to hear your suggestion to improve. We will strive to learn and grow with feedback from our community.

  1. How will the app improve mental health of medicos?

The aim is to help resolve the general underlying issues of the medical community which acts as major stressors. The ecosystem will help deal with burnout and to help formulate better stress coping mechanisms. Every solution, hopefully, will impact the mental health of medicos positively and improve their quality of life.

  1. Why does identification process takes so much time?

The process is done manually in order to avoid unnecessary errors and ensure the accuracy of information so takes more time than usual.

  1. Is medicos connect a corporation?

No, it’s not a corporation.

  1. Are medical students welcome on the app?

Yes absolutely, but if you are under 18, then you won’t be allowed to use the dating module due to underage dating laws in different countries.

Privacy:

  1. Why are there so many identity checks?

Medicos connect uses multiple identity checks to make sure:

  1. true identity of the user

  2. avoid duplicate accounts

  3. avoid cat fishing

  4. avoid scams

  5. no one can harass anyone on theplatform

  6. avoid unsolicited explicit pics on platform

  7. avoid cyber bullying



  1. How will the multiple identification method stated would help us?

Firstly, no one will be able to hide behind the keyboard to launch personal attacks because of their real identities and the fear of being named and shamed in community. Secondly, it will help in tackling the social media toxicity by eliminating trolls which will encourage people to share more.

  1. What if I am hesitant in sharing my personal info?

Your personal data once verified, will be encrypted immediately and will not be accessible to anyone including the developers.

  1. Is my data safe?

Yes, your data is completely safe. We have introduced industry standard security measures in the system.

  1. Can I report someone?

Anyone with malicious intent should be reported immediately by the community members to keep the platform safe.

Q: what steps will be taken to resolve my issue after I report a user?

If it’s a minor issue, then we will warn the user after the review process but if its something severe we will block the user and help you in pursuing legal action

  1. Can’t people misuse this to malign someone or attack someone’s character?

Yes, they can but without proper investigation no steps will be taken against anyone and incase of false accusations the one with false intent will be blocked instead.

Connect Module:

  1. What is “bff” feature in connect?

It’s a feature where you make new friends. As medicos we frequently relocate and have to adjust in new communities all the time. This feature is to help connect like minded people and to establish new connections.

  1. Why is there gender preference option in bff?

It’s entirely up to the users to make friends with whichever gender they preferred or all genders.

  1. I don’t want people close to me, see me on connect?

By default, all your contacts will be blocked by importing/linking your contacts with the app (only if you give permission to the app for accessing your contacts). You can unblock them in the profile settings as per your preference.

Career Module:

  1. What is research partner in career?

The idea is to connect the medicos to conduct research and publish papers. Most students and residents are usually always short on funds but together they can bear the cost of research and publication. Researchers can also benefit by hiring students to aid in their research and thus create a source of income for our young medicos.



Classified Module:

  1. What is the purpose of classified and how does it work?

It’s a P2P system where a person sells directly to another person. The main purpose is to facilitate users with the additional option of buying or selling books, household items, medical equipment and so on.

  1. Do medicos connect charge anything for buying and selling on classified?

No, we don’t charge anything.



  1. Is this the whole ecosystem or will there be other modules as well?

There are few other modules in process. This is just the beginning; the whole ecosystem will be launched in phases in order to not over complicate it for the users.
